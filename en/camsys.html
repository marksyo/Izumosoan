Surveillance Camera Network

We provide design, construction, and maintenance services for surveillance networks, installing multiple cameras indoors and outdoors.

A surveillance camera network consists of cameras, network equipment, servers, and software. It is not a simple installation process.

Considerations such as usage, maintenance costs, and initial investment are essential.

With our expertise in server and network design, we are confident in delivering optimal solutions.

1. On-Site Equipment

Basic Structure of a Surveillance Network

A surveillance network generally consists of three components:

1. On-Site Equipment

On-site equipment includes surveillance cameras, POE network switches, wired LAN cables, and wireless LAN infrastructure.

Typically, SD cards or SSDs are used for data storage.

The primary purpose is to capture the on-site situation.

2. Remote Monitoring

For remote monitoring, at a minimum, an app or website for viewing footage and a data storage solution are required.

Video data can be stored in the cloud, though this incurs monthly fees.

3. AI Video Analysis Software

Beyond viewing footage via an app, software with functions for rapid searching, zooming, and extracting large volumes of image data is necessary.

With advancements in AI technology, image recognition and AI-powered search are now possible.

We offer customizable development to make the software user-friendly for specific purposes.
