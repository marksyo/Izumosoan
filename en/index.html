About Our Name

In Izumo, it is said that in the 10th month of the old lunar calendar, all the gods of Japan gather in Izumo Province.
We aim to pray for safety, act justly, and create a safe society. This is the meaning behind the name "Izumo Souan."

Our Mission

Izumo Souan LLC strives to eliminate "anxiety" and contribute to community safety through technological products and construction services in cameras, communication, and automation systems.
Leveraging years of experience in IT and automation, we provide accessible support services to address technological concerns for small and medium-sized enterprises.
