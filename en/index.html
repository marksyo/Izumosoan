About Our Company Name

In Izumo, there is a tradition that in the tenth month of the lunar calendar (the month of the gods' gathering), gods from all over Japan gather in Izumo Province.
We pray to the gods for safety and aim to create a just and safe society. This is the meaning behind our company name "Izumo Souan."

Our Mission

Izumo Soan LLC aims to contribute to community safety by providing technology products and construction services in cameras, communication, and automation systems to eliminate "anxieties."
Leveraging our long experience in IT and automation, we provide accessible support services to alleviate technological anxieties in automation investments for small and medium-sized enterprises.
