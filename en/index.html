About Our Name

In Izumo, it is said that in the 10th month of the old lunar calendar (Kamiarizuki), gods from across Japan gather in Izumo.
We pray for safety, act justly, and aim to create a secure society. This is the meaning behind "Izumo Soan."

Our Mission

Izumo Soan LLC aims to contribute to community safety by alleviating “anxieties” through technological products and installation services in the fields of cameras, communications, and automation systems.
Drawing on our many years of experience in IT and automation, we provide accessible support services to ease the technical concerns of small and medium-sized businesses investing in automation.
