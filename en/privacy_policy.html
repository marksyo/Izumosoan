Privacy Policy

Izumo Souan LLC (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") receives personal information from many business partners, customers, and employees to achieve its mission of corporate activities and social contribution. Accordingly, we have established the following privacy policy to ensure the proper management and thorough protection of personal information.

  1. For the purpose of conducting business, the Company will clearly define the purpose of use, acquire, use, and provide personal information appropriately, ensure that the acquired personal information is used only within the scope of the stated purpose, and take measures to prevent use beyond that purpose.

  2. The Company will comply with all laws, national guidelines, and other regulations related to the handling of personal information (including specific personal information).

  3. When entrusting all or part of the personal information we handle to a third party, the Company will select companies or individuals that meet our management standards, enter into contracts, and implement appropriate measures and oversight.

  4. The Company will implement safe management and sufficient measures to prevent risks such as leakage, loss, damage, or destruction of personal information, and will take corrective actions as needed.

  5. The Company will respond to requests from individuals regarding the disclosure of their personal information.

  6. The Company will establish a contact point for complaints and address them promptly and sincerely.

  7. The Company will comply with laws related to personal information protection, establish internal regulations, and conduct ongoing reviews. This privacy policy may be revised in accordance with these efforts.

Details regarding the handling of personal information and other related matters are provided on the relevant pages.

Effective Date: May 1, 2025
