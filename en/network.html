Network Design and Construction

1. Network Design

We design and build optimal networks tailored to various environments and purposes, such as data centers, offices, stores, hospitals, factories, and schools. Through timely collaboration between system engineers and construction staff, we provide one-stop network infrastructure solutions.

With our expertise in server and network design, we are confident in delivering the best solutions.

2. Network Construction

We provide network infrastructure for various locations, including data centers, offices, commercial facilities, factories, hospitals, warehouses, university campuses, and regional networks. Our strengths include proposing optimal physical wiring designs and delivering meticulous, rapid responses. We handle everything from CAT6 and CAT6A metal cabling to MTP/MPO, 10G, and 100G optical cabling design and construction.

All construction is performed in-house, ensuring delivery time and quality.

1. Short-Delivery Optical Fiber

We can provide optical fiber with the shortest delivery time. Contact us when you urgently need optical fiber solutions.

2. Optical Fiber Fusion Splicing

When an optical fiber is cut on-site, we can resolve the issue through fusion splicing.

3. Optical Fiber Testing

We own optical loss test sets, enabling rapid quality testing of optical fibers.
