Case Studies

Challenge

A Chinese robot manufacturer won a contract to deploy robots in a Japanese factory, but needed integration with a local conveyor manufacturer. The Japanese customer also required redundancy and 24/7 operational support.

Our Support

Site Survey: Assessed layout, power, and communication environments; defined software customization needs and finalized proposals.
Construction Planning: Designed wiring and scheduling in compliance with Japanese electrical standards.
Installation: Deployed 30 robots, verified communication with upstream systems and conveyors, and performed validation testing.
Operations Manual: Created manuals and conducted on-site staff training.
Ongoing Support: Addressed hardware/software failures and special workflow requests. Developed field tools and trained operators to ensure stable 24/7 production.

Result

On-site deployment completed in 6 months. The customer's 24/7 production ran stably, leading to additional project awards.

Challenge

An American MES vendor entered Japan but had no local technical support staff. Inquiries could only be answered in English, slowing market development. Izumo Soan was engaged when a promising lead emerged.

Our Support

Technical Sales: Trade show support, customer visits, demo presentations, and quotation preparation.
Help Desk: Established a Japanese-language first-contact support system.
On-Site Support: Hardware installation, software deployment, network setup, ERP integration, manual creation, and operator training.
Incident Management: End-to-end triage and vendor escalation.
Pre-Validation: Compatibility testing of new products for the Japanese environment.

Result

Successfully entered the Japanese market. Inquiries from Japan are answered within 8 hours. Customer satisfaction surveys rated support quality as "exceeding expectations," leading to 3 new projects per year.
