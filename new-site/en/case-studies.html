A Global Data Center Operator

Data Center Network Infrastructure Build

Network & Communications Infrastructure

Challenge: A newly built data center in the Greater Tokyo area required high-speed backbone network design and construction between server racks, with 99.999% availability demanded and an aggressive delivery timeline.

Solution: Designed redundant core switch architecture combining Cat6a cabling and single-mode fiber. End-to-end patching for 48 racks with 48-hour burn-in testing to validate all link quality.

Result: Achieved 99.99% availability, completed 1 week ahead of schedule. Ongoing operations and maintenance contract in place.

Scope of Work: Design review, cabling installation, optical loss testing, cutover witness, maintenance handover
"Despite the tight deadline, all test records and handover documents were well-prepared, making post-launch verification easy."Data Center Operations Manager
Duration: 3 months Scale: 48 racks Tech: Cat6a, SMF Availability: 99.99%
An Automotive Parts Manufacturer

Manufacturing Plant IoT Sensor Network Deployment

Field Operations Digitalization Support

Challenge: A Chubu-region automotive parts plant experienced an average of 3 sudden equipment failures per month, each causing approximately 2 hours of production loss. Four facility managers oversaw 200+ machine tools with no centralized monitoring.

Solution: Installed IoT sensors on each machine tool to monitor vibration, temperature, and power consumption. Data was aggregated via LoRaWAN gateways to a cloud platform with automated email alerts for anomaly detection.

Result: Equipment downtime reduced by 30%. Unplanned stoppages dropped to fewer than 1 per month. Manager site patrol time reduced from 20 to 8 hours per week.

Before vs After: Unplanned stoppages from avg. 3/month to <1/month, patrol time from 20h/week to 8h/week
"They adjusted the notification conditions based on how the system would actually be used on-site, so we could immediately integrate it into our maintenance workflow."Director of Equipment, Manufacturing
Duration: 2 months Points: 200 Tech: LoRaWAN Downtime: -30%
A Large Logistics Warehouse Operator

Logistics Warehouse AGV Automated Transport System

Automation System Deployment Support

Challenge: A large Kanto-region logistics warehouse saw inbound/outbound volumes increase 30% year-on-year while facing labor shortages. Forklift and manual transport could not support night operations, causing chronic shipment delays.

Solution: Deployed 12 European-made AGVs integrated with the Warehouse Management System (WMS). Implemented route optimization algorithms, safety barriers, warning lights, and operator training.

Result: Transport efficiency improved by 60%. Enabled 24/7 operations. Shipment delays eliminated and worker workload significantly reduced.

Scope of Work: Wireless site survey, AGV route verification, WMS integration, on-site operator training, safety measures
"What made the biggest difference was that they handled not only the system but also the explanation to site staff as one integrated package."Logistics Warehouse Operations Manager
Duration: 4 months Units: 12 Tech: WMS, AGV Efficiency: +60%
An Industrial Park (Facility Management Company)

Industrial Park Integrated Security System

Security & Fire Alarm Integration

Challenge: A Kansai-region industrial park had 10 factory/warehouse buildings each running different security systems with no unified access control. Fire alarm integration was inadequate, posing regulatory compliance risks.

Solution: Built a centralized management platform integrating existing surveillance cameras, access control, and fire alarm equipment. Connected all buildings via fiber optic backbone. Implemented automated fire department notification.

Result: Initial security incident response time reduced from 15 to 4 minutes on average. Annual security operational costs reduced by 25%.

Before vs After: Initial response from avg. 15 min to 4 min, annual ops cost reduced 25%
"Information that was scattered across different buildings is now centralized in the control room, allowing faster decision-making during night shifts."Facility Management Company Supervisor
Duration: 5 months Buildings: 10 Tech: CCTV, Access, Fire Response: -73%
