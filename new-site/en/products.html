PRODUCT 01

IZUMO SafeOps

Field Safety Operations Coordination Platform

On-site challenges: Information scattered across phone calls, chats, and whiteboards. Field changes cannot be synced in time. Multi-team coordination is difficult. Safety and operational risks are hard to track.

  • Unified visualization of field operation status, work zone status, and risk status
  • Share daily construction and operational impact to reduce information gaps
  • Change notification and confirmation workflow to close the loop on field changes
  • Multi-team field coordination with unified construction, client, and maintenance information

Make field changes visible, share risks in real time, and make operations and construction safer and more stable.

PRODUCT 02

IZUMO SiteBridge

Post-Implementation Operations Improvement Platform for Intelligent Systems

On-site challenges: Only after system acceptance does the real operational environment emerge — congestion, inconsistent processes, opaque issue resolution, and remote teams struggling to understand the field.

  • Full lifecycle management of operational issues, from incident recording to effectiveness verification
  • Visualization of current system status, risks, progress, and operational impact
  • Analysis combining field observations, personnel behavior, traffic peaks, and route conflicts
  • Connect clients, remote technical teams, and field engineers for greater collaboration transparency

Go beyond deployment to continuously improve operations — enabling intelligent systems to truly run stably in the field.
